Meet Bella-The Golden Retriever

Age-2 Years

Gender- Female

Location-Lagos

Health Status-Vacinatted, Dewormed, Healthy

About Bella

Bella is a lovable, energetic 2-year-old Golden Retriever who is ready to bring joy and companionship to her forever home. With her golden fur and soulful eyes, Bella instantly captures the hearts of everyone she meets. She's an affectionate dog who thrives in a family environment and loves being part of daily activities. Bella is great with children, making her the perfect family pet. She has a playful yet gentle demeanor and enjoys spending time outdoors, especially when she gets to run around and fetch her favorite toys. Despite her active personality, Bella knows when to relax and cuddle up, making her a wonderful companion during quiet times as well.

Personality:

  • Friendly & Sociable: Bella gets along well with other pets and humans. She loves meeting new people and will greet you with a wagging tail and plenty of enthusiasm.
  • Playful: She enjoys games of fetch and running around, especially in open spaces like parks.
  • Affectionate: Bella loves attention and will happily lay by your feet or snuggle up on the couch for some bonding time.

Best Home Environment

Bella will thrive in a home with plenty of space to play and a loving family to give her the attention she needs. Whether you have children, other pets, or live an active lifestyle, Bella is adaptable and will fit right in. Her sociable and well-behaved nature makes her an excellent choice for first-time dog owners as well Health & Care: Bella is in excellent health. She has been fully vaccinated, regularly dewormed, and is up to date on all her medical check-ups. She has a clean bill of health from the vet and is ready for adoption.

Feeding:

Bella enjoys a balanced diet of high-quality dog food and occasional healthy treats. Her favorite treats include carrots and peanut butter-filled chew toys. It's important to keep her meals consistent to maintain her health and energy levels.

Exercise Needs

Being an active dog, Bella requires daily exercise to keep her fit and mentally stimulated. She enjoys long walks, playing fetch, and even swimming when possible. A daily walk of at least 30-45 minutes, combined with some playtime in the yard or park, will keep her happy and healthy

Grooming

Golden Retrievers are known for their beautiful coats, and Bella is no exception. She will require regular brushing to prevent her fur from matting and to reduce shedding. Occasional baths, nail trimming, and ear cleaning will keep her looking her best

Adoption Process:

Adopting Bella is easy! Simply click the "Adopt Bella Now" button and fill in a short application form. Our team will review your application and get in touch to schedule a meet-and-greet. If all goes well, you can take Bella home with you to start your new life together

Why Adopt Bella?

Bella is more than just a pet—she’s a loving companion who will bring happiness and warmth into your life. Her playful nature, friendly attitude, and loyalty make her a great addition to any family. Don’t miss the chance to adopt this wonderful Golden Retriever today
